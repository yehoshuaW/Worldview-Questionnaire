Worldview Classification Questionnaire

Traditional Worldview Groupings

1. Do you believe there is God or divine power?

2. Do you believe there is one supreme God who created and governs the universe?

3. Do you believe multiple gods have specific powers or control over certain aspects of life?

4. Do you believe divinity is present in all of nature and the universe?

5. Do you believe that spiritual forces exist independently of any gods?

6. Do you believe in an ultimate, unchanging truth or reality?

7. Do you view humans as having a unique place in the universe, different from other beings?

8. Do you believe rituals or practices are necessary to connect with the divine?

9. Do you believe in life after death?

10. Do you believe there is a sacred, ordered way to live life?

Modern Worldview Groupings

11. Do you believe that science and reason are the primary sources of knowledge?

12. Do you believe that truth is relative and subjective?

13. Do you believe that human consciousness transcends the physical world?

14. Do you believe that morality is a human construct?

15. Do you believe that human life has meaning beyond physical existence?

16. Do you believe that spirituality can exist without belief in a traditional god or gods?

17. Do you view all beliefs and opinions as equally valid?

18. Do you believe that personal experience is more important than objective facts?

19. Do you believe reality is shaped by language and culture?

20. Do you believe that free will exists independently of physical laws?
